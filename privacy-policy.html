Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Selim Yesilyurt ("we", "us", or "our") built the Leben in Deutschland app (the "App") as a free, ad-supported application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

All app data (favorites, question history, exam results, and language preferences) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

2. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display banner and interstitial advertisements in the App. Google AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address
  • General device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy.

3. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS 14 and later, we request your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before allowing advertisers to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites. You can change this permission at any time in your device's Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

If you deny tracking, ads will still be shown but they will not be personalized.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob — for serving advertisements (Privacy Policy)
  • SKAdNetwork — Apple's privacy-preserving ad attribution framework

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Retention

Since all user data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete all app data by:

  • Using the "Reset Statistics" option in the App's Settings
  • Deleting the App from your device

7. Data Security

All locally stored data is protected by your device's built-in security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID). No data is transmitted to external servers by us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising via App Tracking Transparency
  • Reset your advertising identifier in your device settings
  • Delete all app data at any time
  • Request information about your data by contacting us

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
